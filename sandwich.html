How To Make A Sandwich

  1. Gather two slices of bread, meat, cheese, lettuce and mayo.
  2. Spread mayo on both slices of bread.
  3. Layer meat, cheese and lettuce on the first slice of bread.
  4. Place the 2nd slice of bread on top, mayo side down.
